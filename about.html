Our History

Two Decades of Dedicated Education

Established in the year 2005, Shrimathi Ranganayaki Ammal Nursery & Primary School was founded with a profound commitment to serving the community of Pappanaicken Palayam, Coimbatore. We recognised the need for an educational space that balances academic rigour with warmth and stress-free development.

What started as a small nursery school has blossomed into a trusted centre of learning for Pre KG through Std V students. In 2025, under the dynamic leadership of Mrs. Swarna Venkatesh, the school underwent significant infrastructure improvements and curriculum upgrades — introducing STEAM training, Robotics through Lego building, Braindemics visual learning, and dedicated sports programmes.

Over the years, our students have excelled at state-level karate championships, district-level yoga competitions, and participated proudly in international observances including World Yoga Day and World Environment Day.

A Message from Our Director

Our vision for this school has always been rooted in the belief that every child deserves a nurturing, fearless, and joyful start to their educational journey. When I took over the management of Shrimathi Ranganayaki Ammal School, my first priority was to ensure that the 'Mother's Touch' philosophy was not just a tagline, but the very soul of how we operate — from our classrooms to our corridors.

👁️ Our Vision

To cultivate a learning community where child-centric, stress-free, and interactive education guides every student to unlock their highest creative, emotional, and intellectual potential — forming a foundation for lifelong learning and character.

🎯 Our Mission

To deliver a nurturing, happy environment that integrates academic progress, character formation, mindfulness through yoga, and physical strength. We strive to make every lesson an exciting, interactive experience for our students.
