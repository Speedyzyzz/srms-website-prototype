Our History

Over Two Decades of Dedicated Education

Established in the year 1997, Shrimathi Ranganayaki Ammal Nursery & Primary School was founded with a profound commitment to serving the community of Papanaickenpalayam, Coimbatore. We recognized the need for an educational space that balances academic rigor with warmth and stress-free development.

What started as a small nursery school has blossomed into a trusted center of learning for kindergarten and primary standard students. Over the years, we have maintained a solid parent community and have consistently kept classes small to guarantee that every child receives the personal support they deserve.

👁️ Our Vision

To cultivate a learning community where child-centric, stress-free, and interactive education guides every student to unlock their highest creative, emotional, and intellectual potential, forming a foundation for lifelong learning.

🎯 Our Mission

To deliver a nurturing, happy environment that integrates academic progress, character formation, mindfulness via yoga, and physical stamina. We strive to make learning an interactive process that excites every student.
