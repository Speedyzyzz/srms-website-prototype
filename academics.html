Our Approach

Activity-Based & Child-Centric Curriculum

At Shrimathi Ranganayaki Ammal School, our teaching methods are structured around the philosophy that children learn best when they are actively involved in the process. We emphasize discovery, collaborative tasks, and creative expression over mechanical memorization.

Our teachers are trained to offer supportive care, matching lessons to the individual pace of each kid. From fine motor skills development in LKG to basic logic, languages, and environmental science in primary standard 5, we make sure that our curriculum covers cognitive, physical, and emotional growth.
