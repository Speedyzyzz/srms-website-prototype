Our Approach

Activity-Based & Child-Centric Curriculum

At SRN School, our teaching methods are built around the philosophy that children learn best when actively involved in the process. We emphasise discovery, collaborative tasks, and creative expression over mechanical memorisation.

Our teachers are trained to offer supportive care matched to the individual pace of each child. From fine motor skill development in Pre KG to logic, language, and environmental science in Std V, our curriculum ensures holistic cognitive, physical, and emotional growth.
