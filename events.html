Vidyaarambham Ceremony

📅 Date: October 2nd, 2026 (Traditional learning initiation)

Vidyaarambham is a traditional learning initiation ceremony observed during Vijayadashami, where young children are introduced to the world of alphabets, reading, and writing. We invite parents and children to join us in initiating the intellectual journey of our new batch. Special arrangements and enrollment discounts apply for registrations on this day.
