Vidyaarambham Ceremony 2026

📅 October 20, 2026 (Vijayadashami)

Join us for the traditional Vidyaarambham learning initiation ceremony on the auspicious occasion of Vijayadashami. Children are formally introduced to the world of learning — alphabets, numbers, and reading — in a beautiful, celebratory setting. This marks one of the most cherished milestones in a child's early life. All current students and prospective families are welcome.
