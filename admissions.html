Required Checklist

Documents for Admission

Please keep original copies and two self-attested photocopies of the following records ready for submission during your school visit:

  • Child's Birth Certificate

    Issued by Municipal Corporation or Gram Panchayat.

  • Residential Address Proof

    Aadhaar card, utility bills, or voter ID of parent.

  • Parent/Guardian ID Proof

    Aadhaar, Passport, or PAN card.

  • Passport Photos

    4 recent passport-size photographs of the child.

  • Immunization / Medical Record

    Copy of vaccine record card for nursery entries.

  • Previous Transfer Certificate

    Applicable for Standard 2 to 5 admissions.
