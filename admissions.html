How to Apply

Our Admission Process

We make enrolment simple and straightforward for parents. Follow these four easy steps to secure a seat for your child.

1

Enquiry

Submit an enquiry online, call us, or WhatsApp us directly for immediate assistance.

2

School Visit

Schedule a personal visit to inspect our classrooms, facilities, and speak with our team.

3

Documentation

Submit the required documents listed below during your school visit.

4

Confirmation

Pay the initial term fee to confirm your child's enrolment seat.

Required Checklist

Documents for Admission

Please keep originals and two self-attested photocopies of the following documents ready for submission during your school visit:

  • Child's Birth Certificate

    Issued by Municipal Corporation or Gram Panchayat.

  • Community Certificate

    Caste/community certificate as applicable.

  • Address Proof

    Aadhaar card, utility bill, or voter ID of parent/guardian.

  • Child's Aadhaar Card

    Copy of child's Aadhaar card.

  • Parent's Aadhaar Card

    Aadhaar card of father and/or mother.

  • Transfer Certificate

    Required for Std II to V admissions only.
